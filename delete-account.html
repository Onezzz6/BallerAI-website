How to Delete Your Account

Account Deletion Instructions

If you wish to permanently delete your BallerAI account, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your account - Open the BallerAI app and make sure you're logged in
  2. Go to the Profile tab - Navigate to your profile section in the app
  3. Access Settings - Look for and tap on the Settings option
  4. Click Delete Account - Find and select the "Delete Account" option
  5. Authenticate yourself - Complete the authentication process to verify your identity
  6. Confirm deletion - Your account deletion will then be permanent and cannot be undone

Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, you will lose:

  • All your training data and progress
  • Nutrition tracking history
  • Recovery plan recommendations
  • All personal settings and preferences
  • Access to the BallerAI community

Alternative Options

Before deleting your account permanently, consider these alternatives:

  • Take a break: You can simply stop using the app without deleting your account
  • Adjust privacy settings: Review and update your privacy preferences in Settings
  • Contact support: If you're having issues with the app, our support team can help resolve them
